About Us

Dizzle Holy Art mission is to provide a cultural alternative to the commercial art experience, and to celebrate and promote the artistic elements of the medium, and to nurture and promote the art ofitself. The DHA will be the home of DHA, and will provide an unique style of art pursue its mission.Included in the mission will be special consideration for the cultural diversity of the bay area as well as the world, and the DHA will serve as a base for the promotion of the inclusion of international elements, with the ultimate display being The DHA projects. The DHA will become the base for fine arts appreciation for a wide spectrum of local cultural communities andvisitors alike. It will be a place to utilize, enjoy, learn, and discover. It will also be a workplace of inclusion and respect, with a main focus on the development of creativity and ideas.
